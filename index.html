Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How is the insurance premium calculated?

The insurance premium is calculated based on several factors, including the vehicle's make and model, manufacturing year, your driving history, and the coverage options you choose. Insurance companies use complex algorithms to assess risk and determine the appropriate premium.

What type of coverage are available here ?

There are different types of coverage, including liability coverage, comprehensive coverage, collision coverage, and more. Liability coverage typically covers damage to others in an accident, while comprehensive and collision coverage can protect your vehicle from various types of damage.

How does the claims process work here?

In the event of an accident or damage, you should contact your insurance provider as soon as possible. They will guide you through the claims process, which typically involves providing details of the incident, submitting relevant documents, and, if necessary, having an assessment of the damage conducted.

Can I transfer my insurance to new vehicle?

When you acquire a new vehicle, you can seamlessly transfer your existing insurance. Notify your insurance provider about the change, and they will guide you through the process. This involves adjusting coverage and premiums based on the specifications of your new vehicle. It's essential to inform your insurer promptly to ensure continuous and appropriate coverage for your updated automotive asset.
